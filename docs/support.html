Frequently asked questions

How do I save my parking spot?
Tap the blue 'Park Here' button on the map screen. Parked. will save your exact GPS location, reverse-geocode your address, and show it on the map instantly. You can optionally add a photo, nickname, notes and a meter timer during the save flow.
How does the meter timer work?
When saving a spot, set an expiry time on the meter step. Parked. will send you three alerts — at 15, 10, and 5 minutes before your meter expires. Tap 'I'm on my way' on any alert to cancel the remaining ones, or tap 'Add 30 min' to extend your timer.
Why isn't the app finding my location?
Make sure Location Services are enabled for Parked. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Parked. and select 'While Using the App'. If you're indoors or in a parking garage, GPS accuracy may be reduced.
How accurate is the saved location?
Parked. uses your iPhone's built-in GPS. Typical accuracy is within 5–15 metres depending on your device, signal strength, and environment. In underground car parks or areas with poor GPS signal, accuracy may be reduced. Parked. is a memory aid — always take note of visual landmarks too.
I didn't get my meter notification. What happened?
Make sure notifications are enabled for Parked. Go to Settings → Notifications → Parked. and ensure Allow Notifications is on. Also check that your device is not in Focus mode blocking notifications. If you force-quit the app, notifications will still fire from the system.
Can I use Parked. without an internet connection?
Yes. Parked. works completely offline. Map tiles and address lookup require a connection but the app will function and save your spot without one — your coordinates are always saved regardless.
Does Parked. drain my battery?
Parked. only uses location services while the app is open — it does not track your location in the background. Battery impact is minimal.
How do I delete my parking history?
Open the History tab, swipe left on any entry and tap Delete. All data is stored locally on your device and is removed immediately.
